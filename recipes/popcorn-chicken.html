Popcorn Chicken

Homepage

Description

Chicken dish orginated from Taiwan

Ingredients

  • Boneless chicken breast
  • Flour
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Basil
  • Avocado Oil

Steps

  1. Cut chicken breast into small bite-sized pieces
  2. Coat chicken pieces with flour
  3. Deep fry the chicken
  4. Season with salt and pepper and basil.
